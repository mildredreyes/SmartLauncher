# Privacy Policy

Mildred Reyes Petisme respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

SmartLauncher is designed to organize and rank apps on an Android device. To provide its core functionality, the app may access:
- Installed app metadata
- App usage statistics, when the user explicitly grants Usage Access permission

How data is used:
- To classify installed apps into smart groups
- To rank apps based on usage behavior on the device
- To improve the launcher experience locally on the device

Data handling:
- SmartLauncher is designed to process launcher and usage information locally on the device
- No personal information is intentionally sold or shared for advertising purposes
- No sensitive signing, account, or private business materials are included in this public repository

User control:
- Users can choose whether to grant Usage Access permission
- Users can revoke permissions at any time in Android Settings
- Users may uninstall the app at any time
